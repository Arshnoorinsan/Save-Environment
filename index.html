Conservation Tips

  • Reduce, Reuse, Recycle: Minimize waste by following these principles.
  • Save Water: Fix leaks and use water-saving fixtures.
  • Use Renewable Energy: Switch to solar or wind power when possible.
  • Plant Trees: Trees absorb carbon dioxide and improve air quality.
  • Support Local: Buy locally-sourced products to reduce carbon footprint.
